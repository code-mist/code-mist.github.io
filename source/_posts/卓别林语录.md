### 🎭 查理·卓别林经典语录 | Charlie Chaplin Quotes

  1.    **中文**：人生近看是悲剧，远看是喜剧。  

    **English**: Life is a tragedy when seen in close-up, but a comedy in long-shot.

  2.    **中文**：我一直喜欢在雨中行走，那样没人能看到我的眼泪。 

    **English**: I always like walking in the rain, so no one can see me crying.

  3.  

    **中文**：知识让我们愤世嫉俗，聪明让我们铁石心肠。我们想的太多，同情太少。除了机器，我们更需要人性；除了智慧，我们更需要善良。没有这些品质，生命就没有意义。  
    **English**: Knowledge makes us cynical, and cleverness makes us hard-hearted. We think too much and feel too little. More than machinery, we need humanity; more than cleverness, we need kindness. Without these qualities, life will be violent and all will be lost.

  4.  

    **中文**：我的心就如同这张面孔，一半纯白，一半阴影，我可以选择让你看见，也可以坚持不让你看见，世界就像是个巨大的马戏团，它让你兴奋，却让我惶恐，因为我知道散场后永远是有限温存，无限心酸。  
    **English**: My heart is like my face, half white, half shadow. I can choose to let you see it, or I can insist that you don’t. The world is like a great circus; it excites you, but it frightens me, because I know that after the show, there is only limited tenderness and infinite sorrow.

  5.  

    **中文**：世界是一面镜子，你笑它便笑。  
    **English**: The world is a mirror; if you smile, it smiles back.

  6.  

    **中文**：人必须相信自己，这是成功的秘诀。  
    **English**: You have to believe in yourself. That’s the secret of success.

  7.  

    **中文**：有限温存，无限伤感。  
    **English**: Limited tenderness, infinite sorrow.

  8.  

    **中文**：虽然我不是个悲观的人，也不是憎恨人类的人，但是有些时候，与人打交道就让我恶心和抑郁。我发现对生活自己还是个陌生人。发现独处是我唯一的良药！  
    **English**: Although I am not a pessimist or a hater of mankind, sometimes dealing with people makes me nauseous and depressed. I find myself a stranger to life. Solitude is my only remedy!

  9.  

    **中文**：历史上所有伟大的成就，都是由于战胜了看来是不可能的事情而取得的。  
    **English**: All the great achievements in history were made by overcoming what seemed impossible.

  10.  

    **中文**：信仰是人类赖以生存的众多的力量。  
    **English**: Faith is one of the forces by which humanity lives.

  11.  

    **中文**：是要有才智，可是我们更要有仁慈。  
    **English**: We need intelligence, but even more, we need kindness.

  12.  

    **中文**：时间是伟大的作者，她能写出未来的结局。  
    **English**: Time is the greatest author; it writes the ending of every story.

  13.  

    **中文**：贫穷决不是有魅力或可汲取教训的事。对我来说，贫穷只教会我过高地评价有钱人或上流社会的优雅。  
    **English**: Poverty is not charming, nor is it instructive. It only taught me to overvalue the rich and the elegance of high society.

  14.  

    **中文**：对于一个艺术家来说，如果能够打破常规，完全自由进行创作，其成绩往往会是惊人的。  
    **English**: For an artist, if he can break the rules and create freely, the results are often astonishing.

  15.  

    **中文**：什么是生活，生活不过是一种愿望。  
    **English**: What is life? Life is nothing but a desire.

  16.  

    **中文**：在这可恶的世界，什么也无法长久，哪怕是我们的烦恼。  
    **English**: In this hateful world, nothing lasts forever—not even our troubles.

  17.  

    **中文**：世界就像是一个巨大的马戏团，它让你兴奋，却是让我惶恐，因为我知道散场后永远是——有限温存，无限心酸。  
    **English**: The world is like a huge circus; it excites you, but it frightens me, because I know that after the show, there is only limited tenderness and infinite sorrow.

  18.  

    **中文**：无论天资有多么高，他仍需学会了技巧来发挥那些天资。  
    **English**: No matter how high one’s talent is, they must still learn technique to express it.

  19.  

    **中文**：当我真正开始爱自己，我才认识到，所有的痛苦和情感的折磨，都只是提醒我：活着，不要违背自己的本心。  
    **English**: When I truly began to love myself, I realized that all pain and emotional torment was just reminding me: live, and don’t betray your true self.

  20.  

    **中文**：当我开始爱自己，我不再渴求不同的人生，我知道任何发生在我身边的事情，都是对我成长的邀请。  
    **English**: When I began to love myself, I no longer longed for a different life. I knew that everything that happened to me was an invitation to grow.

  21.  

    **中文**：当我开始真正爱自己，我才明白，我其实一直都在正确的时间，正确的地方，发生的一切都恰如其分。  
    **English**: When I truly began to love myself, I understood that I had always been in the right place, at the right time, and everything that happened was exactly right.

  22.  

    **中文**：当我开始真正爱自己，我不再牺牲自己的自由时间，不再去勾画什么宏伟的明天。今天我只做有趣和快乐的事，做自己热爱，让心欢喜的事，用我的方式，以我的韵律。  
    **English**: When I truly began to love myself, I stopped sacrificing my free time or drawing up grand plans for the future. Today I only do what is fun and joyful—what I love, what makes my heart sing, in my own way, at my own rhythm.
