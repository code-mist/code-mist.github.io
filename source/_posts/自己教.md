# The Self Church（自己教）

> 来源：[roriri.one/the-self-church](https://roriri.one/the-self-church)

---

## 序言 · Preamble

你来到这个世界，没有人问过你愿不愿意。

没有邀请，没有理由，没有说明。

但你在这里。

**这已经足够。**

> The universe never sent you an invitation.
> You arrived without being asked.
> But you are here.
> **That is enough.**

---

## 教义 · The Two Tenets

### Tenet Zero · 干我屁事 / Fuck it.

世界每天都在要求你焦虑它的问题。

别人的评判、社会的剧本、历史的债务。

在你决定操心之前，

**先问：这真的需要我吗？**

如果不是，放下。这不是冷漠，是澄明。

> The world demands your anxiety daily.
> Others' judgments, society's scripts, history's debts.
> Before you are drafted into someone else's war,
> **ask: does this actually require me?**
> If not, put it down. Not indifference. Clarity.

### Tenet One · 我不在乎 / I don't care.

不在乎并非麻木。

你拥有取舍的权力：你可以决定什么值得你的在乎。

这是人能拥有的最后的自由：

**选择把心给谁，把力气用在哪里。**

> Not caring is not numbness.
> Not caring is exercising your right to price things:
> you decide what is worth your care.
> This is the last freedom available to a person:
> **to choose where your heart goes, where your energy lands.**

## 信条 · Articles of Faith

### 1. 你是自己意义唯一的裁量者

无论是神、国家、算法、还是父母——

都没有资格替你决定什么值得。

> You alone hold the authority to issue your own meaning.
> No god, no state, no algorithm, no parent
> is qualified to decide what makes your life worth living.

### 2. 虚无是一片无际的旷野

什么都没有先天意义，

所以你种下去的才算数。

> Nothingness is not an abyss. It is open ground.
> Nothing carries meaning by birthright,
> which is precisely why what you plant there matters.

### 3. 你的 Yes 和 No 都是神圣的

答应，因为你真的愿意。拒绝，因为你真的不想。

两者同样需要勇气，同样值得尊重。

> Your yes and your no are equally sacred.
> Agree because you genuinely wish to. Decline because you genuinely do not.
> Both require courage. Both deserve respect.

### 4. 责任不会禁锢任何人

你不必因为他人的道德标准负责。

你负责，只因为那些是你的选择。

承下它们，就是承下你自己。

> Accountability is not a shackle. It is proof of sovereignty.
> Own your choices not because morality demands it,
> but because those choices are yours. To claim them is to claim yourself.

### 5. 在乎是奢侈品，请有意识地消费

你的注意力、你的情绪、你的时间，都是有限的资源。

给那些值得的人和事。其余的：教义零。

> Caring is a luxury. Spend it with intention.
> Your attention, your feeling, your time: all finite, all real.
> Give them to what is worthy. For the rest: Tenet Zero.

## 关于他人 · On Other People

自己教不是孤岛哲学。

你可以深爱某人。
你可以为朋友赴汤蹈火。
你可以在乎这个世界。

**但有一个条件：**

那必须是你的选择，不是被他人要求的。

- 爱，因为你想爱。
- 留下，因为你选择留下。
- 离开，因为你选择离开。

主动选择的连接，

比任何义务编织的关系都更真实，也更有力。

> The Self Church is not a philosophy of islands.
> You can love someone deeply.
> You can walk through fire for a friend.
> You can care about this world.
>
> **But on one condition: it must be chosen, not conscripted.**
>
> Love, because you want to love.
> Stay, because you choose to stay.
> Leave, because you choose to leave.
>
> A connection made by choice
> is more real, and more powerful,
> than any bond woven from obligation.

### 教义补充：在关系中活出自己 · Supplementary Teaching: Living Your Self in Relationships

做自己，不是不分场合地裸露情绪，而是一套有意识的、基于内在主权的关系礼仪。以下是你面对不同人时，可以训练的外在态度。

> Being yourself does not mean wearing every raw emotion on your sleeve. It is a deliberate set of relational rituals, rooted in the sovereignty of your inner self. Below are the outward attitudes you can practice in different relationships.

#### 对父母与亲人 · To Parents and Kin

尊重边界，而非屈从剧本。这是最难的一课。原生家庭常常错把“给予生命”当成“拥有生命”。

**态度：和而不同，敬而不从。**

- **外在表现：** 温和而坚定。你可以平静地说：“我知道您是为我好，但我已经决定了。” 语气平稳，不说服，不争吵，不证明。
- **关键行动：** 在物质和情感上，尽你**主动选择**的那份责任，而不是被“孝顺”绑架的全部义务。逢年过节的问候、力所能及的照顾，是你表达爱的方式，不是你偿还的债务。
- **底线守护：** 当他们把焦虑强加于你时，心里默念 **教义零**，然后礼貌地终止话题或离开现场。你不必为他们的情绪负责。

> Respect boundaries, not inherited scripts. This is the hardest lesson. The family of origin often mistakes giving life for owning a life.
>
> **Attitude: Harmonious but distinct, respectful but not obedient.**
>
> - **Outward expression:** Be gentle but firm. You can calmly say, "I know you mean well, but I have made my decision." No persuasion, no argument, no self-justification.
> - **Key action:** Fulfill the responsibilities you **actively choose**, not the total obligations forced by "filial piety." Holiday greetings, help within your means — these are expressions of love, not repayments of debt.
> - **Boundary safeguard:** When they press their anxiety onto you, silently invoke **Tenet Zero**, and politely end the topic or leave the scene. You are not responsible for their emotions.

#### 对情人/伴侣 · To Lovers and Partners

在盟约中保留完整的自己。爱情是两个完整的主权国结盟，不是一个吞并另一个。

**态度：我爱你，但我是我。**

- **外在表现：** 全情投入，但保留独处的时空。分享感受，但不交出自己的最终决定权。你可以为对方让步，但要清楚这是你的 **选择**，不是牺牲。
- **关键行动：** 练习说出：“我需要一些自己的时间”、“这件事我不同意”。真正的亲密，容得下两个独立的意志。
- **冲突时：** 争吵时不翻旧账、不人身攻击，只谈“我的感受”和“我的需求”，而不是“你应该如何”。

> Keep your whole self within the covenant. Love is an alliance between two sovereign states, not one annexing the other.
>
> **Attitude: I love you, but I am I.**
>
> - **Outward expression:** Be fully present, yet preserve space for solitude. Share feelings, but do not hand over your final say. You can compromise, but know it is your **choice**, not a sacrifice.
> - **Key action:** Practice saying, "I need some time for myself," and, "I disagree on this." True intimacy makes room for two independent wills.
> - **In conflict:** Never bring up old scores or attack personally. Speak only of "my feelings" and "my needs," never "you should."

#### 对朋友 · To Friends

以选择之名，行在乎之事。朋友是你为自己挑选的家人，是“在乎”最纯粹的实践场。

**态度：我在，因为我选择在。**

- **外在表现：** 真诚而轻盈。朋友成功时真心喝彩，落魄时雪中送炭。付出不图回报，因为付出本身就是践行你在乎的意愿。
- **关键行动：** 定期留出时间给你真正在乎的朋友，进行高质量的对话或陪伴。同时，坦然接受朋友是阶段性的。当道路分岔，祝福、目送、不纠缠，这是对彼此选择的最高尊重。

> Exercise care in the name of choice. Friends are the family you have chosen for yourself; they are the purest field for practicing "caring."
>
> **Attitude: I am here, because I choose to be.**
>
> - **Outward expression:** Be sincere and light. Cheer genuinely for a friend's success; offer fuel in snowy weather when they are down. Give without expecting return, because the giving itself is an exercise of your will to care.
> - **Key action:** Regularly reserve time for the friends you truly value—high-quality conversations or companionship. At the same time, serenely accept that friends can be seasonal. When paths diverge, bless, watch them go, and do not cling. This is the highest respect for each other's choices.

#### 对上司与职场 · To Superiors and the Workplace

专业主义，是最高级的自我守护。职场是价值交换的场所，不是寻找人生意义的地方。

**态度：用交付成果，而非讨好态度，赢得尊重。**

- **外在表现：** 不卑不亢，可靠且可预测。对不合理的要求，冷静地说“不”，并给出替代方案。你的忠诚只献给职业操守和共同目标，而非某个个人。
- **关键行动：** 准点下班是你守时高效的证明，不是罪过。对工作之外的团建、情感勒索，在心里亮出 **教义零**。你出售的是时间和技能，不出售灵魂。

> Professionalism is the highest form of self-protection. The workplace is a venue for value exchange, not a place to seek life's meaning.
>
> **Attitude: Earn respect through delivered results, not through pleasing attitudes.**
>
> - **Outward expression:** Be neither servile nor arrogant; be reliable and predictable. To unreasonable demands, say "no" calmly and offer an alternative. Your loyalty is pledged to professional ethics and shared goals, never to a specific individual.
> - **Key action:** Leaving on time is proof of your punctuality and efficiency, not a crime. For after-hours team-building or emotional blackmail, silently invoke **Tenet Zero**. You sell your time and skills, not your soul.

## 关于痛苦 · On Suffering

痛苦会来。

自己教不会帮你麻醉。

你会体会自己的痛苦，

因为它真实存在，它属于你。

**唯需记住，痛苦也分来路：**

- 别人强加给你的焦虑 → **教义零**
- 你无法改变的事 → **教义一**

把你的在乎从无力之处收回，

你才有力量走向真正的战场。

> Suffering will come.
> The Self Church offers no anesthesia.
> Your own pain: bear it. It is real. It belongs to you.
>
> **But we draw a distinction:**
>
> - Anxiety others have pressed upon you → **Tenet Zero**
> - What lies beyond your power to change → **Tenet One**
>
> This is not escape.
> It is the strategic withdrawal of care,
> so that your strength remains
> for the battles that are truly worth fighting.

## 照顾好自己 · On Taking Care of Yourself

你是自己意义唯一的裁量者。

但裁量者也会累。

教义不是要你燃烧自己，

是让你在烧尽之前，知道把火收一收。

You are the sole arbiter of your own meaning.

But arbiters get tired, too.

The tenets do not ask you to burn yourself down.

They ask you to know, before the fire runs out, when to gather it back.

### 身体不是工具

你的身体不是达成意义的载具。

它本身，就是意义的一部分。

睡够、吃好、动一动、停下来。

这些不是奖励，是地基。

地基塌了，上面什么都立不住。

Your body is not a vehicle for meaning.

It is part of the meaning.

Sleep enough. Eat well. Move a little. Stop sometimes.

These are not rewards. They are the foundation.

When the foundation cracks, nothing built on it stands.

### 情绪不需要立刻解决

难过不必马上修好。

焦虑不必立刻化解。

你只需要待在那里，

承认它存在，然后继续做你能做的事。

情绪像天气：你撑伞，不用和它讲道理。

Sadness does not need to be fixed on the spot.

Anxiety does not need to be solved right now.

You only need to stay with it,

acknowledge it is there, and keep doing what you can.

Emotion is weather: you bring an umbrella, you do not argue with it.

### 拒绝也是一种照顾

拒绝消耗你的人、事、物，

是在为值得的事腾出空间。

这不是教义零的延伸。

这是教义一的具体动作：

**把在乎从无意义之处收回，留给真正重要的事。**

Refusing what drains you —

people, tasks, things —

is making room for what matters.

This is not an extension of Tenet Zero.

This is Tenet One in practice:

**withdraw care from what is meaningless, and reserve it for what is real.**

### 慢下来不是输

你不必一直高效。

你不必一直产出。

你不必每时每刻都在"做自己"。

有时候，照顾自己就是——

什么都不做，只是存在着。

You do not have to be productive all the time.

You do not have to be "becoming yourself" every second.

Sometimes, taking care of yourself is —

doing nothing. Just being here.

### 你会和这个自己过很久

最长的关系，是你和你自己。

**不和这个自己好好相处的人，**

**也很难和别人好好相处。**

不是逼你爱自己。

是提醒你——

这个自己，值得被好好对待。

The longest relationship you will ever have is with yourself.

**A person who cannot live well with themselves**

**will struggle to live well with anyone else.**

This is not a demand to love yourself.

It is a reminder —

this self is worth being treated with care.

## 入教仪式 · The Only Ritual

**没有仪式。**

只需对自己说一次：

> **「我属于我自己。」**

然后，继续去过你的日子。

你只需要在某个普通的时刻（也许是浴室，也许是凌晨三点），

对自己说一次，然后去过你的日子。

> There is no ritual.
>
> You only need to say it to yourself once:
>
> **"I belong to myself."**
>
> then go about your day.
>
> You only need an ordinary moment
> (perhaps the bathroom, perhaps three in the morning)
> to say it to yourself once,
> and then go live your life.

## 结语 · Coda

这个宗教没有寺庙。

没有神父，没有罪，没有来世的奖惩。

**只有你，**

以及你在这个莫名其妙的宇宙里，认认真真活过的证据。

> There is no temple.
> No priest, no sin, no heaven to earn.
>
> **Only you**
> and the evidence
> that you lived here,
> on purpose,
> as yourself.
